Overview

Math Drills is built by Skill Joy LLC with privacy by design. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. All app data remains on your device and is not sent to our servers or any third party.

Children’s Privacy

Math Drills is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Choices

You may delete app data at any time by removing it within app settings (if applicable) or uninstalling the app.
